Welcome to Grooce Grocery Store, your one-stop destination for all your grocery needs. Established in 1995, Grooce has been committed to providing our community with fresh, high-quality products at affordable prices. We believe that good food is the foundation of a happy and healthy life, and we strive to make fresh, nutritious, and delicious food accessible to everyone.


Our mission is to offer a wide variety of fresh, organic, and locally-sourced products while providing exceptional customer service. We are dedicated to supporting local farmers and producers, ensuring that our customers receive the best that our region has to offer.


We source our fruits and vegetables from local farms, ensuring peak freshness and flavor. A wide selection of organic products, from produce to dairy and packaged goods. High-quality meats from trusted suppliers, including grass-fed beef, free-range poultry, and sustainable seafood.
